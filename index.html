How to use Puppytask

Alejandra, Co-Founder - Brucira

Start by adding your puppy's daily schedule on Puppytask. Then, everyone can understand the puppy's priorities for the day.

Family/Group members can check off completed tasks as they finish. At the end of the day, it's easy to see what got done and when. The completed tasks will be checked off. Uncompleted tasks are highlighted in red to draw attention. We developed Puppytask a day after getting our office puppy. It decreased our anxiety about getting a new dog and made it easy to give our puppy structure.
